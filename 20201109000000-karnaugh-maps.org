#+title: Karnaugh maps

Karnaugh maps provide a graphical way of minimizing a logic function. The idea is to display the [[id:E23C5DC3-934A-4F55-B401-BE380770C9F3][minterms or maxterms]] in a graph form that allows us to clearly see which variables don't contribute to the output and then omit them.
