#+title: Representation of numbers in Verilog

* Representation of numbers in Verilog
Number can be represented as constants in [[id:73012C9B-F3C0-4379-BA84-831AA20E8F1B][Verilog]], the format is:
\[
\text{<size in bits>'<identifier><digits>}
\]
The indetifier can represent binary (b), octal (o), hexadecimal (h) or d (decimal).
