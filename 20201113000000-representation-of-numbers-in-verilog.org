#+title: Representation of numbers in Verilog

Numbers can be represented as constants in [[id:73012c9b-f3c0-4379-ba84-831aa20e8f1b][Verilog]], the format is:

$$
\text{<size in bits>'<identifier><digits>}
$$

The identifier can represent a binary (b), octal (o), hexadecimal (h) or d (decimal).
