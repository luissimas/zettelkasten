#+title: Karnaugh maps

* Karnaugh maps
Karnaugh maps provide a graphical way of minimizing a logic function. The idea is to display the [[id:E23C5DC3-934A-4F55-B401-BE380770C9F3][minterms or maxterms]] in a graph form that allows us to clearly see wich variables don't contribute to the output and then ommit them.
Honestly there's a lot to cover about Karnaugh maps, so below you can find the PDFs explaining how they work, also check section 2.11 in the [[file:Fundamentals_of_Digital_Logic.pdf.org][book]].
